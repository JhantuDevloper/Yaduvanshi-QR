📘 How to Use the Yaduvanshi QR Tool

Step 1: Upload Your Photo

Click the big "Choose File" button on the home page and select your image. Make sure it's a clear photo.

Step 2: QR Code Gets Generated

Once the image is uploaded, a unique QR code will be automatically created for your image.

Step 3: Share the QR Code

Download or screenshot the QR code. Share it with anyone. When they scan it, they will come to your image page directly.

Step 4: View Image via QR

When someone scans the QR code using their camera or scanner app, it will open your uploaded image in the website page.
